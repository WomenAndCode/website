Women And Code

A non-profit JavaScript learning group for women* in Vienna every second week starting on Sept. 18.

Join us, if you're:
  • thrilled to learn something new
  • interested in coding/programming
  • exited about UI/UX and front-end development
  • eager to learn how to talk to the developers in your company

The first events are for programming beginners - but feel free to join if you want to connect to other amazing people in Vienna!

  • The lectures will be held in English.
  • Participation is for free, but you need to reserve your seat with 9€, which will be transferred back on show-up - if you like, you can also donate this reservation fee or another amount!
  • Please bring your own notebook!

Our Mission

It is important to inspire women to excel in technology careers

Diversity is very important for healthy networks, work relationships and our society in general.
As positive role models we want to inspire other women to join in software engineering.

We want to have an impact on our city

A new non-profit feminist network and meetup/event-series brings more women to tech and encourages women to help other women.
We want to empower women through solidarity, ideas, impulses, network, support, coaching, and sharing of knowledge. time, and space.

FAQ

Why should I join?

It is a great opportunity for you, if you are working in marketing, design, or business collaborating with developers, or if you are thinking about a career change, in order to become independent and make more €€€, specially perfect for (single) mums!

How is teaching women different to teaching men or mixed classes?

Teaching a women-only class is in many aspects different to teaching mixed classes, especially when teaching programming. On the one hand you could have the gender bias from a male instructor and on the other hand having even just one male student in class changes the whole group-dynamic in the classroom: Strong women suddenly become shy and don't dare to ask questions. With Women&&Code we want to offer an open, inclusive, and non-threatening environment for women to learn something male connotated.

How do you define 'women'?

The term 'woman' refers in this context as 'fem*' including people of all colors, shapes, and ages, including but not limited to cis fem, bi, trans, girlies and grannies, tomboys, cowgirls and the like.

The event is full, where is the waiting list?

If the event is fully booked and you missed your spot, please join our group meetup.com/WomenAndCode and we will inform you, when spots become available again.

Also feel free to register for the following event, we will teach lecture 1 and the following in separate groups!

Do you offer childcare?

Please contact us prior to the event so that we can arrange childcare for you!

I like your project, how can I help?

First of all: Thank you!!
And second: You can help us the most through sharing our events! Bring for instance your colleague from marketing, your sister or fellow students and many more to our events.
Alumni students will also be offered the opportunity to mentor new students throughout our event series.
We are also very happy to humbly accept any donations you can give us. <3
